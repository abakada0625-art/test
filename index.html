Process

How AyosPH Works

Simple steps to make your community better.

1

Report an Issue

Take a photo, add details, and submit your report with GPS location.

2

Official Review

Barangay officials review and assign the issue to the appropriate team.

3

Issue Resolution

The assigned team resolves the issue and uploads proof of completion.

4

Verification

Report is marked as fixed and you receive a notification.
